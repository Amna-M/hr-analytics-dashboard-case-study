## Overview
This project involved designing and delivering an end-to-end HR analytics dashboard for a mid-sized energy management organization. The goal was to enable real-time visibility into workforce metrics and reduce manual reporting effort.

## Business Problem
The HR team relied heavily on static spreadsheets and manual reporting, leading to delayed insights, limited trend visibility, and high operational effort. Leadership required a centralized dashboard to monitor workforce performance.

## Key Metrics
- Total headcount and growth trends
- Attrition and turnover rates
- Gender diversity metrics
- Recruitment funnel performance

## Tools Used
- Looker (dashboard development)
- Excel / CSV (data preparation)

## Methodology
1. Collected HR data from multiple internal sources  
2. Cleaned and standardized inconsistent fields  
3. Defined KPIs with stakeholders  
4. Built interactive dashboards with filters  
5. Iterated based on feedback

## Outcome & Impact
- Reduced manual reporting effort by approximately 60%
- Improved access to workforce insights for leadership
- Created a scalable reporting structure for future HR analytics

## Data Disclaimer
All data presented here has been anonymized or simulated to protect organizational confidentiality.
